Face Privacy

Project to detect faces in photos and blur the faces to allow for privacy of individuals.

Two methods are shown, namely the YOLOv3 deep neural network and the Haar Cascades classifier. Both approaches are pretrained on images of faces and show a comparison of how they perform.

Anime Recommendation System

A fun content based anime recommendation system. The animes on myanimelist were scraped on 12/21/19.

The recommender system is initially based on the similarity of genre, type (TV, OVA, etc.), and studio(s). It is then based off the shrinkage estimation of the scores and number of people that scored.
